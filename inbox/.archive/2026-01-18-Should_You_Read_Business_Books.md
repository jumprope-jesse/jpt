# Should You Read Business Books?

## Overview (from Notion)
- Business books can provide insights into effective leadership and management strategies, valuable for running your own company.
- They often offer practical advice on balancing work and family life, which can resonate with your daily experiences.
- You might find unique perspectives on innovation and entrepreneurship that challenge conventional wisdom, inspiring new ideas for your business.
- Some books emphasize the importance of emotional intelligence in the workplace, which could enhance your interactions with employees and clients.
- Alternate views suggest that not all business books are applicable; some argue that real-world experience is more valuable than theoretical knowledge.
- Consider books that focus on technology trends, as they can keep you informed about the latest developments relevant to your software engineering background.
