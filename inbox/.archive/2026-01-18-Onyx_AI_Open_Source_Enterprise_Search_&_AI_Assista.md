# Onyx AI | Open Source Enterprise Search & AI Assistant

## Overview (from Notion)
- Streamlined Workflows: Onyx AI can automate mundane tasks, freeing up your time for family or strategic decision-making as a founder.
- Enhanced Collaboration: With AI-powered search across all company docs and apps, you can easily access information, making meetings more productive and efficient.
- Tailored AI Assistants: Create personalized AI assistants that understand your company's unique knowledge, helping you manage projects and family life more effectively.
- Real-time Syncing: The ability to sync data in real-time means you can access critical information on-the-go, whether at work or while spending time with your family.
- Security: Running Onyx in a secure environment means you can confidently handle sensitive data, knowing it remains within your control.
- Customizability: The open-source nature allows you to adapt the tool to fit your specific needs, whether for business processes or personal projects.
- Empowering Developers: If you have a team, Onyx helps them work faster and smarter, which can lead to innovative solutions and a competitive edge.
- Flexibility in Model Selection: Choose and switch AI models based on your specific use cases, keeping your tech stack relevant and efficient.
- Work-Life Balance: By leveraging AI for information retrieval and task management, you can spend more quality time with your loved ones, reducing the stress of balancing work and family commitments.
- Alternate Views: Consider the potential downsides of relying heavily on AI, such as data privacy concerns, the need for continual training, or the risk of over-automation leading to loss of personal touch in your business interactions.
