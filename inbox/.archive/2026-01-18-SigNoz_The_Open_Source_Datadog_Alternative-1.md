# SigNoz | The Open Source Datadog Alternative

## Overview (from Notion)
- SigNoz offers an open-source alternative to Datadog, which could be a cost-effective solution for monitoring applications, particularly as a company founder.
- The powerful ingestion engine might help in managing large datasets efficiently, essential for scaling your startup.
- With data protection laws in mind, the ability to choose where to store data (US, EU, India) offers flexibility for compliance, crucial for maintaining trust with clients.
- The use of OpenTelemetry for generating logs, metrics, and traces from one SDK simplifies observability and reduces complexity in managing multiple tools.
- The global trust in SigNoz suggests it's a reliable solution, potentially enhancing the credibility of your products.
- Consider alternate views: while open-source is beneficial for customization, it may require more hands-on management compared to fully managed services.
- Reflect on the community aspect; being part of a global network using SigNoz could offer valuable insights and collaboration opportunities.

## AI Summary (from Notion)
SigNoz is an open-source alternative to Datadog, designed for telemetry needs, including logs, metrics, and traces. It features a powerful ingestion engine capable of handling over 10TB of data daily and is used globally by teams of all sizes. Users can choose to self-host or utilize managed offerings to comply with data protection laws in various regions.
