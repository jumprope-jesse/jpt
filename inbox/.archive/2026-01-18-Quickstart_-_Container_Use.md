# Quickstart - Container Use

## Overview (from Notion)
- Embrace containerization to streamline your software projects, ensuring clean environments that avoid conflicts, which is crucial when juggling multiple responsibilities.
- Leverage this technology to enhance collaboration with your team, allowing them to test and deploy features without impacting your main codebase.
- Consider the potential for remote work; containerized environments can simplify onboarding new developers in NYC's competitive job market.
- Explore the sustainability angle: containerization can lead to more efficient resource use, aligning with eco-conscious practices.
- Reflect on the balance between work and family time; container technologies can automate repetitive tasks, giving you more time for personal pursuits.
- Alternate view: while containerization offers many benefits, it may introduce complexity; assess if the learning curve aligns with your current priorities and projects.

## AI Summary (from Notion)
