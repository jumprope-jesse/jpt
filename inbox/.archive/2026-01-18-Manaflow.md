# Manaflow

## AI Summary (from Notion)
- Product Overview: Manaflow is a platform designed to automate repetitive tasks involving data, APIs, and actions with a single click.
- Target Audience: Aimed at businesses looking to scale operations with AI, comparable to well-automated corporations.
- Key Features:
- One-Click Execution: Users can execute workflows easily without manual operations.
- Oversee AI Agents: Users can manage technical workflows while focusing on higher-level automation.
- Templates: Offers a library of commonly used templates for operations, sales, and research to facilitate quick implementation.
- Demos and Learning: Provides live demos and a YouTube channel showcasing how to use Manaflow effectively.
- Support: Encourages users to schedule meetings for assistance with workflow automation or product inquiries.
- Blog: A blog will soon launch to share insights and learnings on AI operations and automation.
- Status: The project is currently in the "Not started" phase as of August 16, 2024.
