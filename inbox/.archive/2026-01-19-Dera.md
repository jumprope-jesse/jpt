# Dera

## AI Summary (from Notion)
- Product Overview: Dera is a single platform designed to manage embeddings and chunks for building production-ready Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) applications.
- Key Features:
- Rapid iteration on chunking strategies to enhance retrieval quality.
- Ability to create different embedding schemas and track matching results.
- Visualization tools for evaluating text and embeddings data.
- Cost Efficiency: Utilizes Neon Database for reduced costs while ensuring scalability.
- Open Source: Dera is completely open-source, allowing users to self-host if desired, while also offering a cloud solution.
- Pricing Model:
- A free tier available for trying out Dera, including 1 project, up to 5 team members, and 20 API requests every 60 seconds.
- Support: Users can reach out directly to a designated contact for inquiries.
- Launch Date: The product was created on February 1, 2024, and is already marked as "Done".
- Website: Available at getdera.com for more information and getting started.

## Iterate on your chunking strategy rapidly

A good RAG system depends on good retrievals, and good retrievals depend on good chunking strategies that result in quality embeddings. With Dera, you can

1. Create different embedding schemas easily
1. Track matching results
1. View the results in the app
## Visualize and evaluate

Inspired by Jerry Liu's (CEO & Cofounder of LlamaIndex) talk, and frustrated by our own experience when building RAG applications, we build this tool to help visualize and evaluate text and embeddings data.

## Reduce costs while ensuring scalability

Dera is built on Neon Database, which allows us to reduce costs while ensuring scalability for our users.

## Completely open-source

Dera is completely open-source and you're free to self-host. Check out our Github for more information. Our cloud offering is always available if you prefer to save the hassle.
