### Risks and challenges

The primary risk to the project is Apple's app review process. Since this is an iOS app, it will be subject to their review and approval, and it will be their decision whether or not to allow it in the App Store. However, we have successfully launched many apps and are aware of the limitations and restrictions. We will do everything possible to make sure the app passes review. Software development is always a challenge and time estimation is doubly so. We'll try to hit the target date, but there's always a chance (probably a good one) that it'll take a little longer than we thought. That said, the Iconfactory has an excellent reputation for shipping quality products and doing it on time.
