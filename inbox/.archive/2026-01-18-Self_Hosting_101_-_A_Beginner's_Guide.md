# Self Hosting 101 - A Beginner's Guide

## Overview (from Notion)
- Self-hosting can empower you to take control of your digital life, enhancing privacy and security for your family.
- As a software engineer, you can explore new technologies and tools, fostering creativity and problem-solving skills.
- Running your own services can save costs and provide unique customization options for your personal or business needs.
- It encourages a hands-on learning approach, perfect for teaching your kids about technology and responsibility.
- Consider the time investment versus the benefits; some may argue that using established services is more efficient.
- Explore the balance between self-hosting and convenience, particularly in a fast-paced city like NYC.
- Community engagement through local tech meetups can provide valuable insights and networking opportunities.
