# Sandstorm Features

## Overview (from Notion)
- The Sandstorm platform simplifies app management, streamlining your workflow as a software engineer and founder by integrating various tools in one place.
- Single Sign-on eliminates password fatigue, allowing you to focus on building your business without the hassle of managing multiple logins.
- The unified sharing access control could enhance collaboration with your team, making it easier to share documents and manage projects efficiently.
- Security features ensure your sensitive data remains protected, which is crucial for maintaining client trust and safeguarding intellectual property.
- Automatic updates mean less maintenance on your part, allowing you to dedicate more time to innovation rather than upkeep.
- The ability to back up your data easily is essential for peace of mind, especially if you’re managing critical business information.
- Unique to Sandstorm, the "Powerbox" UI allows for secure, intuitive interactions between apps, which could inspire new ways of integrating tools in your projects.
- Consider the alternative approach of traditional app management, where security and integration can become cumbersome—Sandstorm's model could be a refreshing change.

## AI Summary (from Notion)
