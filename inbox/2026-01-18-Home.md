## AI Summary (from Notion)
Stravu offers a collaborative platform where users can edit AI-generated content, including text, tables, diagrams, and formulas. It features tools for team communication, confidence scoring for AI outputs, and project context management, all while ensuring data privacy by not training on user data. Users can approve changes, manage notebooks, and leverage AI agents for research and organization.

Inline Edit Text

Prompt to generate text. Edit that text manually or have AI edit it. Build a document with AI that knows your business.

Prompt to generate a table. Manually edit the cell contents, rows, columns. Have AI help edit or do more research. Filter. Arrange. Leverage the table in your notebook and next prompt.

Prompt to generate a diagram. Edit the diagram manually or have AI edit the diagram. Refer to the diagram in your notebook and future prompts.

Turn your research and analysis into a two by two comparison that is editable by you and AI. Leverage it in the notebook and future prompts.

Use AI to generate computational formulas based on your context. Edit and have the computations automatically re-run. Leverage it in your notebook and future prompts.

Notebook AI Agents

As you and your team work in your notebook, chat with an AI Agent. It can research across the web and your documents and update your notebook.

## Diffs with Approval

Stravu will highlight AI additions in green and AI cuts in red. You can review this, edit it directly, and when you are ready approve the change.

Confidence Scoring

Worried about hallucinations? Stravu AI rates its confidence in its research, hiding results its not sure of. Ask for more research for low confidence answers. Dig in, verify, edit yourself.

Fully collaborative AI chat threads with multiple team members

Multi-person Doc Edits with AI

Turn any chat into a notebook. Edit, write, and update inline with your team and AI at the same time.

Collaborate with Agents

Prompt agents to work in a document, do research, organize across your documents, and find conflicts.

Projects give Context

Projects are groupings of notebooks, uploaded documents, websites. Stravu AI understands this context and leverages it to assist you.

Search and Recents

Search your workspace for keywords, chats, notebooks, and projects. Find your Recent Files and see what teammates are working on.

Reusable AI prompts

Stravu is hosted and runs out of Amazon AWS. We retain your data in AWS only to provide you our service. Stravu does not train on your data or prompts.
