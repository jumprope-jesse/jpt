# Frigade AI - User Onboarding Platform

AI-powered platform for user onboarding, in-app guidance, and product adoption.

**URL:** https://frigade.ai/

## What It Does

- **Onboarding automation**: AI analyzes your product to document key workflows automatically
- **In-app guidance**: Product tours, tooltips, and contextual help
- **Surveys & forms**: Timely, non-intrusive user feedback collection
- **AI support assistant**: Resolves common questions before they reach support
- **Feature promotion**: Nudges for upsells, cross-sells, and feature discovery

## Key Value Props

- Replaces multiple tools (onboarding, surveys, support) with one platform
- No-code/low-code - product teams can launch without engineering on every iteration
- AI auto-generates product tours without manual configuration
- Cross-functional: sales (account enrichment), marketing (updates), product (feature promotion)

## Notable Users

- Arc Browser - uses it for launching native experiences

## Considerations

- Balance automation vs. personal touch for complex queries
- User analytics can inform product decisions
- Potential for revenue increase through feature discovery
