# OSINT Techniques and Indicators

Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) methods for gathering insights from publicly available information.

## Unconventional Indicators

### Pentagon Pizza Index
- **URL**: https://www.pizzint.watch/
- **Concept**: Tracks pizza delivery patterns to the Pentagon as a proxy indicator for military tension/activity
- **Rationale**: When staff work late during crises, pizza orders spike
- **Source**: PizzINT (a blend of "Pizza" and "SIGINT/HUMINT" intelligence terminology)

This exemplifies how everyday commercial data can serve as unconventional signals for significant events - a form of "lifestyle OSINT" that correlates mundane activities with geopolitical developments.
