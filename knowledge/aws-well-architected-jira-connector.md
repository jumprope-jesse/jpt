# AWS Well-Architected Tool Connector for Jira

Connector that creates a closed-loop mechanism for managing and tracking risks identified through AWS Well-Architected reviews.

## What It Does

- Generates Jira tickets directly from Well-Architected Tool findings
- Sends updates back to Well-Architected Tool as tickets are resolved
- Includes notes from Well-Architected in Jira tickets for context

## Benefits

- Streamlined workflow from risk identification to remediation
- Reduces manual effort marking Best Practices as complete
- Automatic sync keeps both systems in alignment

## Availability

Available in most major AWS regions including US East/West, Canada, Europe, Asia Pacific, South America, and Middle East.
