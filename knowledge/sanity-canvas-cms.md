# Sanity Canvas

Headless CMS with creative content authoring platform.

## Canvas Feature

- Distraction-free creative writing space
- AI ghostwriter assistance - follows your lead, provides suggestions on demand, steps aside when you take control
- Seamless hand-off to structured content
- Best-practice templates for quick app setup

## Use Cases

- Content creation with AI assistance to overcome writer's block
- Collaborative content workflows
- Structured content management with creative authoring
