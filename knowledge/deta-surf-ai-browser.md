# Deta Surf - AI-Powered Research Browser

**URL:** https://deta.surf/
**Status:** Evaluating
**Category:** Personal Knowledge Management / Research Tools

## Overview

Deta Surf is an AI-powered browser designed to generate personal notes from various media. It combines browsing with a notebook interface, aiming to reduce context switching between consuming content and taking notes.

## Key Features

- **Auto-Generated Notes**: Creates summaries from YouTube videos, PDFs, and web content
- **Deep Dive Capability**: Get both quick summaries and detailed breakdowns of content
- **Notebook Interface**: Built-in note-taking alongside browsing
- **No-Code Visualizations**: Create interactive graphs and applets without coding
- **Multi-Format Support**: Works with web pages, YouTube, PDFs, foreign languages, scientific diagrams
- **Integrated Experience**: Explore web content, videos, and PDFs without leaving your notes

## Design Philosophy

- **Private and Open**: Designed to be "radically personal" while remaining connected to the web
- **Less Friction**: Reduces scrolling, typing, and copying compared to traditional browsers
- **Context Preservation**: Minimizes switching between consumption and note-taking modes

## Use Cases

- Condensing hours of YouTube videos into digestible notes
- Summarizing lengthy PDFs for research
- Understanding complex scientific diagrams
- Learning foreign languages with AI assistance
- Visualizing data without coding skills
- Maintaining focus during work by reducing context switching

## Comparison Notes

Similar to mymind in the AI-assisted note/knowledge capture space, but:
- Full browser vs bookmarking tool
- Focused on real-time summarization vs after-the-fact organization
- Interactive notebook interface vs visual collection
- Emphasis on research workflow vs idea capture
