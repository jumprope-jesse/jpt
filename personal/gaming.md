## Browser Games

### Red Alert 2: Chrono Divide
- **URL**: https://chronodivide.com/
- **What**: Fan-made browser recreation of Command & Conquer: Red Alert 2
- **Status**: Playable with full multiplayer support and all original maps
- **Features**:
  - Runs entirely in browser (no plugins/installation)
  - Cross-platform: works on desktop, mobile, tablets
  - Aiming for feature parity with vanilla RA2
- **Discord**: https://discord.gg/uavJ34JTWY
- **Notes**: Good for nostalgia gaming with kids - classic RTS with strategy/teamwork lessons
