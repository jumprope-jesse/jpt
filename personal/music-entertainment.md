### Jazz Bass Essential Listening
- **Source**: [NYT "5 Minutes That Will Make You Love Jazz Bass"](https://www.nytimes.com/2024/05/01/arts/music/jazz-bass-music.html)
- **Curators**: Working jazz bassists and music writers
- **Recommended Tracks**:
  - "El Haris (Anxious)" by Ahmed Abdul-Malik - East meets West fusion, album "Jazz Sahara"
  - "But Not for Me" by Ahmad Jamal Trio (Israel Crosby on bass) - iconic bass line, "At the Pershing" album
  - "Christina" by Buster Williams - from "Something More" (feat. Herbie Hancock, Wayne Shorter)
  - "For Love (I Come Your Friend)" by Thundercat - George Duke cover, "The Golden Age of Apocalypse"
  - "Yesterday Princess" by Stanley Clarke - 1974 self-titled album, bass as melodic storyteller
  - "Jive Rhapsody" by Duke Ellington Orchestra (Jimmy Blanton on bass) - pioneering bass work
  - "Boomerang" by Marcus Miller - from "M²" album (2002 Grammy winner)
  - "Gloria's Step" by Bill Evans Trio (Scott LaFaro on bass) - "Sunday at the Village Vanguard"
  - "Choma (Burn)" by Harold Land (Reggie Johnson on bass) - 1971, bass as anchor in chaos
  - "Cherokee" by Christian McBride Trio - "Live at the Village Vanguard"
  - "Lonesome Lover" by Max Roach (Art Davis on bass)
  - "Charlie M" by Art Ensemble of Chicago (Malachi Favors on bass) - Mingus tribute
- **Key Artists**: Charles Mingus, Ron Carter, Israel Crosby, Buster Williams, Scott LaFaro, Christian McBride
- **Insight**: "While it might be the most unheralded of all the instruments, no composition resonates without it"
- **Added**: 2026-01-18

### 2024 Oscar Documentary Nominees
- **Source**: [The Verge - Stream the 2024 Oscar documentary nominees](https://www.theverge.com/24084812/the-oscars-academy-awards-2024-documentary-nominees-streaming)
- **Context**: Nominees reject mass-appeal celebrity docs in favor of innovative storytelling and underrepresented voices

**Four Daughters** (Best of the bunch)
- **Streaming**: Available for rent
- **About**: Tunisian family relives trauma by casting actors to share screen and burden - blurs line between performance and reality
- **Style**: Inventive - uses artifice of film as medium, similar to *The Act of Killing*
- **Why watch**: Finding truth through the tension between performance and reality

**Bobi Wine: The People's President**
- **Streaming**: Disney Plus
- **About**: Ugandan pop star turned activist's fight to unseat 40-year dictator; co-director Moses Bwayo was shot while filming
- **Themes**: Democracy, press freedom, resilience under autocracy
- **Related**: *Navalny*, *A Thousand Cuts*

**20 Days in Mariupol**
- **Streaming**: PBS (free)
- **About**: AP journalists document Russia's invasion as only international press left in city
- **Note**: Graphic but essential - captures reality of war that autocrats want hidden

**To Kill a Tiger**
- **Streaming**: Netflix
- **About**: Indian farmer fights for justice after his 13-year-old daughter's assault
- **Note**: Director requests viewers respect privacy of family - no identifiable photos

**The Eternal Memory**
- **Streaming**: TBD
- **About**: Chilean journalist who covered Pinochet's dictatorship navigates Alzheimer's with his partner
- **Themes**: Love story, memory, caregiving balanced with creative practice
- **Related**: *American Symphony* (Jon Batiste)

- **Added**: 2026-01-19

### Objectified (2009) - Free to Watch
- **Director**: Gary Hustwit
- **URL**: https://www.ohyouprettythings.com/free (free screening for 15th anniversary)
- **Runtime**: 75 minutes
- **Documentary about**: Our complex relationship with manufactured objects and the designers who create them
- **Featured designers**:
  - Jonathan Ive (Apple)
  - Dieter Rams (Braun - see "10 Principles" philosophy)
  - Naoto Fukasawa, Marc Newson, Karim Rashid
  - Paola Antonelli (MoMA), IDEO, Smart Design
  - Ronan & Erwan Bouroullec, Hella Jongerius, Alice Rawsthorn
  - Chris Bangle, Tim Brown, Dunne & Raby, Jane Fulton Suri, Rob Walker
- **Key Questions**:
  - What can we learn about ourselves from the objects we surround ourselves with?
  - How does design shape identity and experience?
  - What's the role of sustainability in design choices?
  - How do we balance functionality vs. aesthetics?
  - What does consumerism mean in an age of minimalism?
- **Why watch**:
  - Relevant to tech founder perspective: intersection of technology and design, software enhancing physical products
  - Design thinking for everyday life - from gadgets to spaces
  - Understanding user experience beyond the screen
  - Connection to Dieter Rams' principles (likely referenced in product design discussions)
- **Related**: Director also made "Eno" (currently screening)
- **Added**: 2026-01-19
